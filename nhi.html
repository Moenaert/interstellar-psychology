Non Human Intelligence

David Grusch is a former U.S. Air Force officer and intelligence official who served as a representative to the Unidentified Aerial Phenomena Task Force (UAPTF) and co-led unidentified anomalous phenomena (UAP) analysis at the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA). He held top-secret security clearance but was not a high-ranking military officer; his role was more operational within intelligence analysis. In 2023, Grusch made headlines as a whistleblower, testifying before Congress that the U.S. government operates secret "legacy programs" to retrieve and reverse-engineer non-human craft and "biologics" (biological remains) from UAP crash sites, claiming these involve non-human intelligence (NHI) and have been concealed from oversight bodies.

Karl Nell is a retired U.S. Army Colonel who served as the Army's liaison and executive director for the Pentagon's UAPTF, as well as director of a related UAP office within the Army. As a senior military officer, he reached the rank of Colonel, indicating significant leadership experience in aviation and intelligence. Nell has publicly asserted there is "zero doubt" about the existence of NHI on Earth, stating that non-human entities have been interacting with humanity for some time and that continued government secrecy risks a "catastrophic" uncontrolled disclosure. He co-authored reports and papers, such as "The UFO Enigma: A Call for US Government Disclosure," advocating for transparency on UAP and NHI.

Jay Stratton is a former high-ranking civilian intelligence official who served as the director of the UAPTF, leading U.S. government investigations into UAP for 16 years. He held a senior executive service (SES) position, equivalent to a general officer in influence, overseeing operations across the Department of Defense (DoD) and intelligence community. Stratton has disclosed his direct involvement in confirming evidence of NHI through UAP investigations, breaking his silence in interviews, a forthcoming memoir, and the 2025 documentary The Age of Disclosure, which alleges an 80-year U.S. cover-up of UFOs and alien encounters. He describes the stigma around UAP as a national security threat.
